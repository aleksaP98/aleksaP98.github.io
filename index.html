Furniture Factory Predragović was founded in 1990 in Belgrade as a factory for the production of upholstered furniture. The intensive economic, technological and investment development of the company began in 1994 with the construction of a modern factory for the production of upholstered furniture with state-of-the-art equipment and technology.
